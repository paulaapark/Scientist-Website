Nigel Braun

Nigel was born and raised in Montreal. He completed his B. Sc. in Biochemistry at McGill University in 2014.
After finishing his degree, he worked as a chemistry laboratory technician for several months.
In Januray 2015 he started his M. Sc. at McGill under the supervision of Dr. Bruce Arndtsen.
The focus of his research is the Pd-catalyzed intramolecular cyclization of iminium salts.
When not in the lab, he enjoys traveling, going to the gym and photography.
